ABOUT ME

Hi, my name's Tung, at the time of posting this post, I'm currently a sophomore of AI major in University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam National University, Hanoi (UET-VNU). I was born on October 12th 2004, on a beautiful island. This website is where I'm going to share my learning journey and even my future career path, very looking forward to see my improvement everyday.

I started this path just about almost 1 year ago, when I just got into college, I still remember how I didn't like it at first, it was hard and too complicated for me whom although do really well at high school and got a pretty decent result on the national exam. I spent a lot of times playing video games in Freshman year and just felt lost when my classmate solving hard problems on class everyday. At that time, playing video games made me feel so guilty as my parents are working so hard to give me the best education I could have, I thought I really needed to sit back and question myself, and thankfully, I did. I fixed myself, I began to like coding, started to study, watched lots of Youtube Coding videos and still trying to improve everyday.

I have experienced working with Python(self-taught), C++, Java(school) and many frameworks. I have also tried some online Data Science course. With that being said, I considered myself as lack of skills so I create this website so that I can get myself motivated in order to expanding my knowledge and skills. Let's start!!!
